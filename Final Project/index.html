ALL FILM TYPES

We are a film lab that specializes in developing and scanning film.

We offer services for color, black and white, and slide film as well as prints for 35mm and medium format film.

COLOR - 35MM & 120MM FILM

Color Film Development, Scanning, and Prints.

35mm film is printed on 4x6 luster paper.

Medium formats (6x4.5, 6x6, and 6x7) is printed on luster paper without any cropping.

MONOCHROME - 35MM & 120MM FILM

Black and White Film Development, Scanning, and Prints.

35mm film is printed on 4x6 luster paper with no color cast.

Medium formats (6x4.5, 6x6, and 6x7) is printed on luster paper without any cropping.

E6 SLIDE FILM

E-6 Slide Film Development, Scanning, and Prints.

35mm film is printed on 4x6 luster paper.

Medium formats (6x4.5, 6x6, and 6x7) is printed on luster paper without any cropping.
